# Tony Voice Notes — One Pager
**23-24 April 2026 | 4 voice notes, ~45 min | Source: Gary Duffy (Discover Holidays) + Tony**

---

## The Client
- US HNW, $125K+ household income, bucket-list trip, 4-12 month lead time
- Wants: **expert local knowledge, attentiveness, credibility, someone who cares**
- Clusters in states with direct NZ air routes: NY, CA, TX, WA, AZ
- Top motivators (~70% each): landscapes, adventure, safety, wildlife, culture, "can't get elsewhere"

## The Website
- **1.52 seconds** to engage — hero image is everything, don't clutter, mobile-first
- Make awards **bigger** — 2026 World Travel Awards finalist x2 (Tour Operator + DMC)
- Drop generic trust badges; keep only meaningful credentials
- Key messages: NZ-only specialist, family-owned, NZ-owned, "we care about you"
- Always offer a path to a **real human** — phone, callback, or scheduling

## The Sale
- Target **50% inquiry-to-booking** conversion
- **Phone is king** — email is fallback. Cover US time zones
- Trust must be established **immediately** — slow response = lost client
- AI concierge is valuable but must always offer human handoff
- Clients want to feel guided, not sold to — personal connection, banter, genuine interest

## The Product
- **Modular itineraries**: templated core, customised per client
- **Freedom of choice menu**: daily activity options so clients feel in control
- Mid-trip check-in goes a long way and is remembered
- Supply categories: lodges, heli, private guides, activities, internal flights, driver/transport

## The Numbers
- ~**$50K NZD** per journey (direct)
- **35-40% margin** target (direct); ~25% via agency
- Cost split: 50% accommodation/F&B, 25-30% transport, rest activities/heli

## The Documents
- Proposal PDF (fast to produce, flexible to rearrange)
- Detailed itinerary, confirmation, pre-departure pack — all PDF

## The Credibility (New)
- **2026 World Travel Awards finalist** — NZ Tour Operator + NZ DMC (two categories)
- **Australian trademark** for "Curated Experiences" accepted (TM)
- Qualmark, Travel Life sustainability, Tech NZ — all in progress
- Cruise heritage: **Silversea, Celebrity, Ponant** VIP land programs
- PPG Tours = Best NZ DMC, World Travel Awards 2025

## The Team
- Sales stays in sales; **travel designers** own the client relationship on-ground
- Travel designers are incentivised, driven by genuine interest
- Team page: first names, real experience, human photos

## Referrals > Repeat
- NZ = once-in-a-lifetime for most — repeat unlikely, **referrals are the growth engine**
- Post-trip surveys to capture feedback and encourage word of mouth
