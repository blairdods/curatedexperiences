The listing of high end luxury lodges and hotels that have earned at least one Michelin Key.

## Two Michelin Keys ("An Exceptional Stay")

These properties are recognized as true destinations in their own right, offering exceptional character, architectural grandeur, and world-class service within an authentic New Zealand setting.

- **Huka Lodge (Taupō)** — *The historic lodge is the pioneer of the NZ luxury lodges.*

- **The Lindis (Ahuriri Valley, Southern Alps)** — *A futuristic nature lodge famous for its sweeping, low-profile architecture that blends seamlessly into the Ahuriri valley.*

- **Rosewood Cape Kidnappers (Hawke's Bay)** — *A unique and spectacular cliffside retreat set on an 800-acre working farm.*

- **Rosewood Kauri Cliffs (Matauri Bay, Northland)** — *An exquisite property with panoramic ocean views and championship golf overlooking the Bay of Islands.*

- **Blanket Bay (Glenorchy / Lake Wakatipu)** — *The quintessential rugged alpine-wilderness escape, with a unique charm and heritage.*

- **Otahuna Lodge (Tai Tapu, near Christchurch)** — *A meticulously restored historic Victorian mansion famed for its historic potager gardens and estate dining.*

- **Wharekauhau Country Estate (Palliser Bay, Wairarapa)** — *A sophisticated Edwardian country house set against a dramatic, windswept coastline and wildlife.*

## One Michelin Key ("A Very Special Stay")

These properties deliver distinct personality, unique layout/design, and hospitality that regularly goes the extra mile.

### Auckland & Surrounds

- **The Hotel Britomart (Auckland CBD)** — *NZ's first 5-Green Star hotel, praised for its eco-conscious design and urban craft.*

- **Park Hyatt Auckland (Wynyard Quarter)** — *Premium waterfront luxury with massive rooms and elite harbor views.*

- **Hotel Fitzroy curated by Fable (Ponsonby)** — *A moody, high-end boutique villa stay in one of Auckland's trendiest inner suburbs.*

- **Delamore Lodge (Waiheke Island)** — *Perched on the cliffs with a world-famous view looking back across the Hauraki Gulf.*

### Queenstown & Central Otago

- **Eichardt's Private Hotel (Queenstown)** — *An iconic lakefront historic gem right in the beating heart of town.*

- **Rosewood Matakauri (Queenstown)** — *Stunning, design-forward lake and mountain luxury facing the Remarkables.*

- **Azur Lodge (Queenstown)** — *Freestanding villas capturing uninterrupted, floor-to-ceiling views of Lake Wakatipu.*

- **Stoneridge Estate (Lake Hayes, Queenstown)** — *Boutique vineyard lodging with striking stone architecture.*

- **Gibbston Valley Lodge and Spa (Gibbston)** — *An upscale retreat entirely integrated into the local winemaking region.*

### Regional New Zealand

- **Eagles Nest (Russell, Bay of Islands)** — *Multi-award-winning private luxury villas sitting high on a ridgeline.*

- **Solitaire Lodge (Lake Tarawera, Rotorua)** — *A peaceful, historic lakeside lodge deep in geothermal and cultural heartland.*

- **Bay of Many Coves (Marlborough Sounds)** — *A remote coastal hideaway accessible only by boat or helicopter.*
